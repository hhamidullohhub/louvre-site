Las Meninas is a 1656 painting by Diego Velázquez, the leading artist of theSpanish Golden Age.

It was cleaned in 1984 to remove a "yellow veil" of dust that had gathered sincethe previous restoration in the 19th century.

The cleaning provoked furious protests, not because the picture had been damagedin any way, but because it looked different.
